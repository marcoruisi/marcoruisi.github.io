Field Notes

The pages on this website didn’t all begin in the same way.

What I now think of as a principle almost always started as something much smaller.

A question.

A detail that kept coming back to me.

A solution discovered almost by accident.

Or an idea written down before I knew whether it really mattered.

Over time, some of those observations found their way into real projects.

The ones that kept proving useful gradually became part of the way I design.

That’s where the Principles came from.

In a way, this is their natural path.

Field Notes → Stories → Principles → Manifesto

Field Notes are where everything begins.

Stories show what happens when an idea meets the real world.

Principles are what remains after that idea has been tested over and over again.

The Manifesto is the broader view that emerged from all of it.

This isn’t a blog.

It’s a notebook.

And it will probably never be finished.
